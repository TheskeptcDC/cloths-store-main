JOHM's PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy describes how JOHM's Boutique ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit or make a purchase from JOHM's.Com (the "site"). We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

When you use this website and/or purchase goods

We collect the following data:

  • Name
  • Address
  • E-mail address
  • IP Address
  • Purchasing history and behavior
  • Credit card details

Your details are collected when you browse with us and place an order.

When you browse our website

We don't collect any personal data except for what your browser automatically transmits. We also collect how you use the website and which products you view.

This data is processed based on legitimate interests according to Data Protection Act No. 3 of 2021 Zambia, to optimize the website, ensure security, and identify misuse. Data is not shared with third parties and is stored for no longer than 14 months.

When you order online

We collect identification and contact information when you order, such as name, address, phone number, and email address.

This information may be shared with third-party payment providers and carriers to fulfill your order.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies remember your actions and preferences. You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in practices or for legal reasons.

Contact

For more information about our privacy practices, please contact us at Johnsonlineboutique@gmail.com.

If you have a complaint, you can lodge it with the relevant Data Protection Authorities.
